It is late at night in New York, and Danny Devito is chasing Steve Harvey in his car, with burrito in the driver's seat. When Steve tries to turn, the car gets stuck in an Olive Garden parking lot. Irritably, he snaps at the waitress to hand over her camera, which she does after rummaging through her bag. Steve's furry face takes a illegibly blurry photo of Danny's license plate.

Taco Bell has traced Danny's license plate number to an empty alley in Chicago. When he demands to know why she hasn't come back yet, one of his cats nervously shows him the latest news headline: "Danny Devito has Burrito". Steve is enraged, because lawyers.
